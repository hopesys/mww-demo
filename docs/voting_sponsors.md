# VOTING and SPONSORS

## 1. VOTING: Global vs. Thai

The voting systems for the international and Thai stages share some similarities but have distinct operational differences, particularly regarding payment methods and contestant identification.

### **A. Miss Wellness World (Global Stage)**
*   **Concept:** Voting is designed to allow the audience to support representatives from over 30â€“40 countries.
*   **Mechanism:** The proposed system involves users selecting the **photo of the contestant** along with their **country** on the official website.
*   **Payment & Logistics:** The exact system for international money transfers was still under determination.
*   **Website Requirements:** The website development plan suggests a need for a "Voting System" that could potentially include purchasing voting points (via Credit Card or QR Code) and might display real-time scores (Leaderboard).

### **B. Miss Wellness World Thailand (MWWT)**
*   **Concept:** This voting system is specifically for the 30 finalists chosen from the audition rounds.
*   **Mechanism:** Voters select a contestant's photo, which is assigned a specific code ranging from **MWWT01 to MWWT30**.
*   **Process:**
    1.  **Selection:** Choose the image/number of the contestant.
    2.  **Payment:** Perform a money transfer (specifics on "transfer steps" and "how to check balance" are part of the system design).
    3.  **Closing Time:** The voting system is scheduled to close **1 day before the Final Round** to allow for the clearing and calculation of scores.
*   **Side Stages:** The "Angel of Wellness" (Kids 8-12) and "Princess of Wellness" (Teens 14-17) pageants utilize a similar voting system to MWWT, featuring photos and personal information.

## 2. SPONSORS and PRODUCTS SOLD

The sponsors for Miss Wellness World 2025 span various industries, focusing heavily on health, beauty, wellness, and lifestyle products.

### **Key Sponsors & Partners**

*   **Decathlon (Thailand)**
    *   **Role:** Presented the *"Grace in Swimwear"* award.
    *   **Products/Services:** A global sports brand described as meeting the needs of every sports enthusiast. They provided the swimwear for the competition.
*   **So Cute Clinic**
    *   **Role:** Presented the *"Miss Luminous Complexion"* award.
    *   **Products/Services:** Aesthetic and beauty clinic services.
*   **Cleo Clinic Aesthetic Center Co., Ltd.**
    *   **Role:** Represented by President Cleopatra Akaranari (honored as a Leader/Goddess of Wellness).
    *   **Products/Services:** Leading aesthetic clinic renowned for clear, flawless, and glowing skin treatment (acne, freckles, and dark spots).
*   **Saffron Laboratories Co., Ltd.**
    *   **Role:** Presented the *"Miss Beaming Charm"* award.
    *   **Products/Services:** Preserving and sharing Thai heritage, specifically involving the timeless wisdom of **Thai traditional healing**.
*   **New You Life Thailand**
    *   **Role:** Represented by General Manager Satian Pakun.
    *   **Products/Services:** Described as a **"hormones health company,"** focusing on life balance.
*   **Roy Gem Gallery**
    *   **Role:** Presented the 3rd Runner-up award.
    *   **Products/Services:** Gems and jewelry.

### **Other Mentioned Entities & Supporters**
*   **Grand Better International:** Modern female leadership and career building.
*   **Marin Times:** National-level Buddhist and art exhibitions, Chakra healing, and spiritual wellness.
*   **Local Cultural Products:** The event heavily promoted **Lanna traditional fabrics**, **Lac-dyed fabrics**, and **ceramic herbal compress balls** from Lampang and Chiang Mai as part of the wellness culture segment.
