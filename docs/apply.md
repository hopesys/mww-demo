## Miss Wellness World Thailand (MWWT)
**"Beauty with Wellness"**
We are looking for women who are ready to be the "Wellness Ambassador" for Thailand—ladies who possess beauty of the body, mind, and spirit to drive the "Wellness Nation" movement.

### **Candidate Qualifications**
*   **Gender & Nationality:** Female by birth, Thai nationality, holding a Thai National ID card.
*   **Age:** 18 – 32 years old (as of the application date).
    *   *Note: Applicants under 20 must have parental consent.*
*   **Marital Status:** Single, never previously married (legally or de facto), and never given birth.
*   **Attributes:**
    *   Good personality, intelligent, and good interpersonal skills.
    *   Ability to use **Social Media** effectively to build a personal brand.
    *   Physically and mentally healthy.
*   **Prohibitions:**
    *   No history of drug use or smoking.
    *   No history of posing for nude, semi-nude, or indecent photographs/media.
    *   No criminal record or involvement in illegal professions.
    *   Not a bankrupt individual.

### **Required Documents for Application**
To complete your application, you must prepare the following digital files:
1.  **Introduction Video:** Length not exceeding **2 minutes**.
2.  **Photos (No Filter/No Makeup App):**
    *   Straight face (Close-up)
    *   Left profile & Right profile
    *   Half-body
    *   Full-body
3.  **Activity Photos:** Photos demonstrating social work or volunteer activities.
4.  **Application Fee Slip:** Proof of payment for the application fee (to be attached to the form).

### **Application Process Steps**
1.  **Online Registration (Direct Application):**
    *   Fill out the personal information form on this website.
    *   Upload the required documents (Video, Photos, Payment Slip).
2.  **Confirmation:**
    *   Once submitted, you will receive a **PDF Application Form** via email confirming your submission.
3.  **Audition & Selection Timeline:**
    *   **Application Period:** Open Now – **31 August 2025**.
    *   **Round 1 (Screening):** Selection from online applicants (approx. 500 candidates) down to **120 candidates**.
    *   **Round 2 (Audition):** Selection from 120 candidates down to **30 candidates** (from the Direct Application pool).
    *   **Boot Camp & Preliminaries:** The 30 Direct Applicants will join 30 Provincial License Winners (Total 60 contestants) to compete for the Final 30 spots.
    *   **Finalist Announcement:** 6 September 2025.
    *   **Orientation (Online):** 8 September 2025.

## **Miss Wellness World (MWW) - International**
**Represent Your Country on the Global Stage**
Miss Wellness World is an international platform searching for role models in wellness from over 30-40 countries.

### **For Candidates:**
*   **Qualifications:** Women aged **18 – 32 years** who embody the "Beauty with Wellness" concept.
*   **How to Apply:** International candidates are selected through **National Directors (ND)** in their respective countries.

### **For National Directors (ND) & License Inquiries:**
We are inviting organizations and leaders globally to become **National Directors** and send a representative to Miss Wellness World 2025.
*   **Application Process:**
    1.  Contact the Miss Wellness World Organization via email to express interest.
    2.  Eligible partners will receive an official **Google Form** to complete the license application (sent privately upon discussion).
    3.  Once confirmed, the Country and Candidate profile will be featured on our official site.
